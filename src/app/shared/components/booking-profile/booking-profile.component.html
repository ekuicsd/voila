What you'll do

The experience begins by meeting at the scenic lake in the evening as the sunsets. After meeting the other members of your group you will gear up for an evening paddle board. A short safety and "how to paddle board" instruction session follows before you make your way along the shorelines of the pristine lake to a cave filled with Glowworms. Entering the cave is like entering a magical world as the glow worms surround you on all sides. Enjoy the magic before exiting the cave and on a clear night gazing at the stars on your return paddle home.
