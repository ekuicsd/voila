FAQs

Tourists

Bookings

  • How to make a booking on Voila?
    To book a Voila tour, follow the below stated easy steps:
    • Select your destination and suitable date to travel and search for all the deals and guides,
    • Filter your search according to preferable prices, ratings and more,
    • Then you have two alternatives,
      • Either Pick one of deals that you like and confirm the date, timings and enter the number of people for your booking and click the button "Book now" and get your tour booked.
      • Or Pick one of the guides according to your preferences, visit his/her profile and click on the "Request a personalized tour" button. Then enter all your requirements and send the booking request to the guide. The guide will soon respond and you both will get to chat in our chatbox and hence finalise the booking.
    • You can clear all your doubts regarding your tour by discussing with your guide via the Voila chat. For deal tours, the tourists will be contacted in a group chat named as the tour's name.
  • Where do I find my booking details?
    Your booking details can be found under the Bookings section of your Voila profile when you are logged into the website or the app. Once you click on the bookings section, you can find all your current tour bookings, past bookings and personal tour requests there each in a seperate tab. This section also has a link to the chat section which allows you to communicate directly with your host.
  • What if I have special needs for my experience?
    You should inform your tour guide about your requirements as soon as possible in your chat section. This shall allow the guide to customize the things the way you want.

Guides

Basics

  • Who can become a Voila host?
    Voila is a platform to enable people share their love for their city and earn through the same. Requirements for our hosts:
    • One can be a full-time or part-time guide as per their availibility.
    • The one showing on the profile and tour on Voila. This person will always be the one providing the experience/tour and services and is the person who interacts with our guests.
    • As a guide, you are committed to represent Voila while interacting with the guests.
  • What services does Voila offer me as a Guide?
    Voila gives guides a platform to offer experiences to travellers from around the world. To enable travelers to find hosts and have an excellent customer experience, Voila delivers several services:
    • An easy to use platform to receive and manage bookings.
    • Outstanding customer service for hosts and travellers.
    • Promotion of our hosts and experiences on the platform.
  • Hosting requirements
    The following requirements are minimum standards that hosts must fulfill to keep a Voila profile.
    • Being available and reachable by both the guests and the office, in particular for any urgent or time sensitive issue, and around the time of a booking.
    • Respecting the commitment that is a booking. Unforeseen situations can of course happen, but being late, cancelling or not showing up to a booking without informing your guests and without a valid reason is not acceptable
    • Avoiding any inappropriate behaviour such as smoking during a tour.
    • Remain impartial and keep a service mind and a calm temper when interacting with guests
    • Informing guests of relevant safety procedures when necessary, keeping the relevant emergency numbers and being aware of the closest medical facilities is a must.
    • Possession or use of illegal drugs on tour will result in instant removal from Voila platform.

Your Account

  • How do I create my account?
    You can explore all Voila deals and guides without signing up. However, it is necessary to sign up to complete a booking by creating an account. You can easily create an account by filling up some details in the register section- there you can register as a tourist or a guide, as per your will. Once you sign up and login, your profile will be created where you can view all the related information.
  • How to change my password?
    To do so, first login to your account and then click at an icon in the top-right corner and select "Change password" option there.
  • How do I edit my account settings or profile?
    You can directly manage your personal information on the "Edit profile" page. After logging in to your account, you will find that option in a dropdown by clicking at an icon in the top-right corner.

    • 1.5 METRE DISTANCE: Hosts should respect the 1.5-meter distance from the guests. This includes no handshaking with the guests at the beginning or end of the tour. A simple wave üëã or a bow üôè goes a long way.
