A local guide hiring platform

Nobody knows a location better than its local residents. Voila is an app which harnesses the same fact and connects a traveller to his/her compatible guide who shares interests like food, history, architecture, etc. so that the travelling experience can become even better while giving a significant boost to freelancing opportunities in the tourism industry.
